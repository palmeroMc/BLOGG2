Sikmura pa ba 'to?

by Mary Christelle Palmero

     Never in my life na nasabihan akong maarte sa pagkain. My fam will only call me out whenever I eat weird food combinations. "Ano bang klaseng sikmura meron ka?" Kahit ilang food combos na yung nagagawa ko, madalas gulat parin sila. In the end, nginingitian ko na lang sila.

     When I was a kid, I tend to pair my rice meals with hot drinks lalo na kapag breakfast. Hindi lang 'to drink' but to pour it on rice making it sinabawan meal. Seems bearable naman para sa lahat kaso it came to a point na hindi lang sa rice nahahalo yung mga drinks. I remember soaking Cream-O cookies sa orange juice, I was delightful. My sister called me, and asked me why would I do that. Yes it's kinda gross to imagine, but in my defense, soaking cookies in juices is same thing as eating them separately, mine's only faster. IFYKYK.

     My favorite foods to eat are chicken and tuna dishes. Usually, the chicken is paired with ketchup/gravy/mayo/toyomansi if it's fried or roasted. However, kapag ang ulam namin ay tinola, my taste buds seem to ask for more flavor. Don’t get me wrong, I’m not a picky eater nor my mom’s cooking is terrible, I just have weird food pairings. The missing flavor that my tongue needs for my Tinola is something I can get from soy sauce… Not fish sauce or chicken cubes–soy sauce. I like to eat the chicken bits dipped in soy sauce before feasting on its broth and Sayote. I may have call it Tinola Pro Max. Next food combo I’d like to mention is my go to tuna rice meal. Spicy San Marino corned tuna with a sweet twist. I rarely eat spicy corned tuna on its own, due to my low spicy tolerance, kapag naubusan ng red, no choice na but to go for the spicy one. It’s not that bad pero I knew it can be a better meal. That’s when I discovered adding enough amount of milk (Bear Brand for best combo) is the one that’ll make the meal to the next level. Not too much of it but just enough to cover the rice and tuna. After trying that, dumalas na yung cravings ko sa meal na yun. I have plenty of combo meals pero those two are my topmost favorites.

     To some, it is challenging for their tummy situation to try or experiment new food combos that’s why most of the time it’s easy for them to judge a food combo by its look only. I have experienced some trouble na rin naman pero rarely. Nevertheless, I, myself is the one I need approval for, no other than. As long as it satisfies me and it doesn’t affect my stomach, I couldn’t care less to what other people think of my food combos. My sikmura, my choice.
